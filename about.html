Erica Whyte

Front End Developer

Github

Erica is a Front End Developer with 5+ years experience.

She is proficient in HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery. Erica has also worked within a variety of tech stacks including ASP.NET, Struts, CodeIgniter and Laravel and a variety of content management systems including Sitefinity, Sitecore, Adobe CQ (UI only) and EpiServer. She's familiar with accessibility standards and have worked on sites that required AA standards or higher.

She’s worked for McCann (formerly MacLaren McCann) and Mirum on brands like General Motors, Bayer Crop Science, Wind Mobile, MasterCard, TD Bank, Adidas and Danone. After working with agencies, she moved client-side to a saas company working on their marketing team.

Erica built, trained and lead and 8 person content team to launch 16 sites in under a year for her company's largest client. She's been the SEM for CASL, emails and content management systems.
